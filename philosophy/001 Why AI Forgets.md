# 001 Why AI Forgets

AI forgets because generated insight is not automatically owned.

A model can produce useful reasoning during a task, then lose that reasoning
when the context disappears.

ATLAS treats this as an architectural failure, not a UX inconvenience.

Forgetting is not merely the absence of stored text.

Forgetting is the failure to turn experience into improved understanding.

## Principle

```text
Do not preserve everything.

Preserve what was understood.
```

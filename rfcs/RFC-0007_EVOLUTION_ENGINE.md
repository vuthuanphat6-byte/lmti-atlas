# RFC-0007: Evolution Engine

Status: Draft

## Question

How does ATLAS improve after every completed task?

## Scope

This RFC will define how verified experience becomes safer, deeper and more
reusable cognition.

## Required Design Pressure

The RFC must answer:

* What qualifies as an experience?
* What evidence is required before learning?
* How are bad conclusions rejected?
* How is sensitive experience protected?
* How does evolution reduce repeated reasoning?
* How are regressions detected?

## Non-Goals

* blindly learning every output,
* overwriting stable knowledge without evidence,
* storing sensitive experience outside the privacy layer.
